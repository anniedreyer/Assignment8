This summer, I went on a roadtrip with my friends
to explore the American Southwest.

Below are select images taken on the trip.

Throughout our 5 day trip, we traveled from Kansas City to Texas, New Mexico, and Colorado.
We drove to Amarillo on the first night, White Sands the second night, Santa Fe for two nights,
and Colorado Springs for our final night. In sum, it was a glorious five days spent listening to all of
the old jams, eating way too much fried food, and me making my two friends pull over way too
many times so I could take photos. There was a surplus of caffeine involved as well.
